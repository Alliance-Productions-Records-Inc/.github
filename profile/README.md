## BeatPass

### [beatpass.ca](https://beatpass.ca)

**BeatPass is AP’s flagship platform for modern beat discovery, licensing, producer growth, and music workflow infrastructure.**

BeatPass is not just a beat library. It is a full-stack music platform designed to improve how creators move from discovery to decision, from upload to protection, and from license to verification.

### Key innovations behind BeatPass

- **Modernized beat licensing**
  - BeatPass issues license certificates with public verification links, status checks, usage terms, and fingerprint-linked certificate data.

- **Audio fingerprinting built into the workflow**
  - Uploaded tracks are fingerprinted to detect duplicates, support uniqueness checks, and strengthen later license verification.

- **Fingerprint-aware certificate infrastructure**
  - BeatPassID connects license records to analyzed audio and can surface fingerprint status, analyzed duration, and fingerprint hash in certificate tools.

- **Database-backed license verification**
  - Public verification pages confirm whether a certificate exists, whether it is valid, and whether it matches BeatPass records.

- **Audio Recon for rights investigation**
  - Verified producers can detect high-similarity matches between their tracks and songs on supported streaming platforms, helping surface possible unauthorized use for review.

- **Beat Requests**
  - BeatPass modernizes custom beat sourcing through structured request briefs, timed submission windows, producer submissions, and request management workflows.

- **Producer intelligence**
  - Dashboards, analytics, XP systems, achievements, leaderboards, and progress views give producers clearer visibility into momentum, activity, and growth.

- **Commerce and payout infrastructure**
  - BeatPass supports exclusive licensing, Stripe-powered purchase and payout flows, and producer finance workflows designed for real monetization.

- **Security and developer readiness**
  - BeatPass has documented API authentication, rate limiting, protected licensing and fingerprinting endpoints, and reduced exposure of sensitive fingerprint and user data.
